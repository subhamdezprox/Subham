### SEO Naming Best Practices
- **Use hyphens** to separate words (not underscores or spaces)
- **Keep it descriptive** but concise (under 60 characters)
- **Include brand name** (shubham) for brand recognition
- **Use relevant keywords** (sustainable, construction, natural, eco-friendly)
- **Use lowercase letters** consistently
- **Avoid special characters** and numbers unless necessary
- **Include location context** where relevant (exterior, interior, hero)
